Frequently asked

Quick answers.

A few of the things people ask before getting in touch — in case yours is here.

Do you take referrals from schools or social workers?

Yes — we work alongside schools, social workers, and health professionals all the time. Get in touch and we'll set up a conversation about how we can support together.

How quickly will I hear back?

Within two working days. If it's urgent, please call.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, we welcome volunteers — especially with experience in education, SEND, childcare, or community work. PVG checks and induction training are mandatory; we'll guide you through it.

Do you accept donations?

We do, and every penny goes back into the work — funding subsidised places for families who need them. Mention donations in your message and we'll send you details.

I'm a researcher / journalist / partner organisation — who do I speak to?

Use the form above and put your role in the subject line. We'll route it to the right person.
