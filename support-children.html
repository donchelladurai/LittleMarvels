Support for children & young people.

From early-years through to teenage years — programmes that meet each child where they are, and grow with them as they do.

Every child is met as an individual first, with their additional support needs as context — never as the whole story. Our sessions are designed by practitioners trained in trauma-informed care, early intervention, and SEND best practice.

Sessions run weekly through term time, with seasonal extras during school holidays. Spaces are funded where possible to keep cost from being a barrier.

Our programmes

What's on offer.

Weekly Targeted Support Sessions

Personalised, small-group sessions that address each child's specific developmental, social, and sensory needs — designed in conversation with caregivers.

Creative Arts Workshops

Painting, music, movement, and craft — encouraging expression and giving every child a way to share who they are without needing words.

Active Play & Sports

Inclusive movement sessions promoting physical health, social interaction, and teamwork — adapted so every child can take part on their own terms.

Vocational Skills Workshops (Teenagers)

Practical, hands-on workshops building skills for future independence — from cooking and money handling to interview practice and workplace readiness.

Sibling Bonding Events

Time and space for siblings to play, laugh, and be siblings — strengthening bonds and giving brothers and sisters their own moment in the spotlight.

Inclusive Open Days

Drop-in days that bring families together for fun, low-pressure activities and the chance to meet others walking similar paths.

Family & Carer Support

Coffee mornings, support groups, and workshops for parents and carers.
