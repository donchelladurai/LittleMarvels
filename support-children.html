Inclusive Nursery, Early Years & After-School.

A nurturing, safe, inclusive setting for children with additional support needs — structured developmental activities, peer socialisation, and the flexibility every family deserves.

Every child is met as an individual first, with their additional support needs as context — never as the whole story. Our sessions are designed by practitioners trained in trauma-informed care, early intervention, and SEND best practice.

Sessions run weekly through term time, with seasonal extras during school holidays. Spaces are funded where possible to keep cost from being a barrier.

Our programmes

What's on offer.

Structured Developmental Activities

Daily routines designed by ASN-trained practitioners — language, motor, social, and pre-academic learning, paced to each child.

Safe, Structured, Inclusive Spaces

Sensory-considered rooms with quiet zones and active zones — built so every child can find the kind of space they need that day.

Family Communication Pathways

Daily handovers, regular check-ins, and shared planning — caregivers stay in the loop and shape what happens next.

Emotional & Developmental Support

Trauma-informed practice underpins everything — co-regulation, gentle scaffolding, and time and space to grow.

Peer Socialisation & Engagement

Small-group activities that scaffold friendship, turn-taking, and shared play — at each child's pace, never forced.

Flexible Routines & Accommodations

Schedules and supports adapt to the child — sensory breaks, alternative communication, individual plans, and after-school continuity.

Vocational Skills Workshops (Teenagers)

Practical, hands-on workshops building skills for future independence — cooking, money handling, interview practice, and workplace readiness.

Home & Family Support

Home interventions, respite, parent & carer training, and signposting.
