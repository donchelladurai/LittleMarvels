CreateAble Hub.

A creative & sensory development space — where children and young people of all abilities discover what they can do, express what they feel, and find joy in the doing.

CreateAble Hub is the creative arm of Little Marvels — a dedicated space designed around sensory, expressive, and adaptive activities. It exists alongside our programmes for those who want a regular creative practice, and for those who just want to come and try something new.

Sessions are led by practitioners trained in inclusive arts and sensory integration. Pace, materials, and group size are tailored — there is no "right way" to take part.

Image — creative session in progress
What's inside

Six ways to create.

Activities are layered so a child can step in at any level — sensory exploration one week, group performance the next.

Creative Exploration

Open-ended studio time with paint, clay, mark-making, and mixed media — process over outcome, with no pressure to "finish".

Sensory Integration Activities

Tactile, visual, and auditory experiences designed by practitioners trained in sensory regulation — calm corners alongside high-energy zones.

Social Inclusion & Group Sessions

Small-group activities that scaffold social connection — turn-taking, collaborative making, shared performance — at each child's pace.

Expressive Arts & Movement

Music, dance, drama, and storytelling — embodied ways to communicate without needing words, and to build coordination and confidence together.

Cognitive & Adaptive Skill-Building

Structured activities that strengthen problem-solving, sequencing, and everyday-life skills — designed to feel like play, not therapy.

Inclusive Community Workshops

Open-to-all themed workshops — seasonal making, sensory storytelling, family art days — bringing the wider community into the Hub.

What participants gain

More than the making.

Confidence

Trying, failing, trying again — in a space where there is no wrong answer.

Discovery

Finding out what the body, the voice, and the hands can do — sometimes for the first time.

Connection

Making alongside others, sharing the room, finding small moments of common ground.

Expression

Tools and time to communicate the things words don't always reach.

Skills

Coordination, sequencing, sensory regulation — built quietly in the doing, not taught at.

Joy

The reason for all of it — the laughter, the mess, the small wins, the long absorbed silences.

How to take part

Ways in.

CreateAble Hub is open to families directly — and the income from drop-ins, memberships, and workshops keeps the space sustainable and our other programmes accessible.

Sponsored places are available for families who couldn't otherwise attend. No one is turned away on grounds of cost — please ask.

Drop-in Creative Sessions

Pay-as-you-go sessions throughout the week.

Membership Packages

Regular access to all weekly sessions, with discounts on workshops.

Sponsored Places

Grant-funded places for families who need them.

Workshops & Community Events

Themed standalone sessions open to all, all year round.
