Accessibility & sensory

What our space is like inside.

Step-free throughout, with a quiet sensory room available at all times for anyone who needs a break from noise or activity.

Lighting is soft and adjustable. We have ear defenders, fidget tools, and visual schedules on hand. Accessible toilets with changing facilities are on the ground floor. Service dogs are welcome.

If you'd like to visit ahead of bringing your child for a first session — to walk the space and meet the team — please get in touch and we'll arrange it.

First visit

What to expect.

A few things families have asked us before coming in. If your question isn't here, just drop us a line.

Do I need to book before turning up?

For coffee mornings and inclusive open days you can drop in. For weekly support sessions, workshops, and one-to-one programmes, please get in touch first so we can prepare a tailored session for your child.

What should we bring on a first visit?

Comfortable clothes, any sensory aids your child usually uses, a snack and drink. We provide everything else needed for the session.

Will I be expected to stay for the session?

For first visits, yes — we ask a parent or carer to stay so the child has a familiar anchor. After the first few sessions, many families step out for a coffee while the child settles into the routine. Always your call.

How do you handle behaviour or sensory difficulties?

Our team is trained in trauma-informed and sensory-aware practice. We don't aim to suppress behaviour — we aim to understand what each child is communicating and respond to it. If a session becomes overwhelming, the sensory room is always available.

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies by session type and is flexible. We actively seek grant funding to subsidise places for families who can't afford private services. Please ask — no one is turned away because of cost.

Is there parking on site?

Yes — free parking with accessible bays close to the entrance and a drop-off zone right at the front door.

Want to come for a look first?

No commitment. We'll show you around and answer whatever you'd like to ask.
