What we believe.
What we’re here for.

A short articulation of why Little Marvels exists, what we’re reaching for, and the values that hold the whole thing together.

Our Mission

To provide inclusive, nurturing, and high-quality education, interventions, and family support that empowers everyone to thrive — and helps families feel seen, supported, and strengthened.

We work with children and young people with additional support needs and the families around them. Every programme is shaped by the family it serves, not the other way around. We start with what each child can do, what each family needs, and we build outwards from there.

Our team is trained in trauma-informed care, early intervention, and SEND best practice. We complement existing services rather than duplicate them — strengthening the network already around a family rather than asking them to choose.

Our Vision

A community where every individual is recognised as a marvel.

Where every family is uplifted, and no one is left behind — regardless of ability, background, or circumstance.

We envision a future where Little Marvels grow into confident, joyful individuals who know their worth and shine in their world.

Our values

Six pillars.

Not slogans on a wall. Working principles we test ourselves against.

Identity

Every individual is unique, valuable, and full of purpose. We start with the child, never the diagnosis.

Inclusion

Spaces where all abilities and backgrounds are celebrated and supported. No one performs to belong.

Love

Care rooted in compassion, kindness, and respect — for children, families, and each other.

Excellence

High standards in everything — from early education to safeguarding to staff development.

Growth

Holistic growth — physical, emotional, social — and walking alongside families through it.

Community

Bridges, not walls. Partnerships with parents, schools, and local services that extend care.
