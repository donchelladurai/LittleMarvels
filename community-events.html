Community events.

Joyful gatherings throughout the year — open to families, siblings, friends, and neighbours. Belonging happens at celebrations.

Our community calendar is built around the rhythm of the year. Spring brings Bloom & Blossom. Summer brings the Splash. Christmas brings carolling and the Bonanza. In between, regular open days and inclusive sessions for anyone who wants to drop in.

Every event is designed with sensory considerations baked in — quiet rooms, flexible timings, low-pressure formats — so families don't have to ask for accommodations to feel welcome.

Annual events

Things to look forward to.

Bloom & Blossom (Spring)

Our springtime celebration for children with additional support needs and their families. Gardening, crafts, music, and a fresh start to the year together.

Summer Splash

An afternoon of inclusive water play, garden games, and picnic lunches — with quiet zones and shaded breaks for anyone who needs them.

Inclusive Open Days

Regular drop-in days for any family wanting to see what we do, meet our team, and let the children play.

Sensory Christmas Carolling

Festive visits with carols adapted for sensory comfort — bringing the warmth of Christmas to families in their own spaces.

Christmas Bonanza

Our flagship festive event — entertainment, food, gifts, and the kind of celebration where every family feels squarely included.

Sibling Bonding Days

Special days dedicated to siblings of children with additional needs — fun, friendship, and time to be themselves.
